The Dhāraṇī for Retaining the Perfection of Wisdom in a Hundred Thousand Lines 
Homage to the Bhagavatī, the Perfection of Wisdom. 
tadyathā | oṃ munidharme saṃgrahadharme vimuktidharme sadā anugrahadharme vaiśravaṇa parivartata | dharme sarvakārya paripramāṇadharme samatāparivartanadharme svāhā | oṃ prajñā śruti smṛti vijaye dhīḥ dhāraṇīye svāhā | 
By taking up this dhāraṇī, one will retain The Perfection of Wisdom in a Hundred Thousand Lines. 
This completes “The Dhāraṇī for Retaining the Perfection of Wisdom in a Hundred Thousand Lines.”
