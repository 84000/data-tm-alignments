
The Dhāraṇī to Uphold “The Noble Great Amulet” 
Homage to the Three Jewels!
namaḥ samanta­buddhānām aprati­hataśāsanānāṃ oṃ maṇidhari vajriṇi mahāpratisare hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ svāhā!
By upholding this, one will have upheld The Noble Queen of Incantations: The Great Amulet.
Here ends “The Dhāraṇī to Uphold ‘The Noble Great Amulet.’ ”
