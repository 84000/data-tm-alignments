The Noble
Essence Dhāraṇī of Śākyamuni
Homage to the thus-gone, worthy, fully and perfectly awakened Śākyamuni!
tadyathā| muni muni mahā­munaye svāhā
When one has recited this once, whatever negative actions one has committed during eighty trillionkalpas, will all be purified. 
The Noble Dhāraṇī of the Essence of Śākyamuni is complete.
