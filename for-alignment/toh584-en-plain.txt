The Dhāraṇī for Retaining the Noble Avatamsaka Homage to the Three Jewels. 
oṃ nthiṃ | nāmaḥ sarvabuddhānām | oṃ māṃ bhrūṃ mūṃ | tadyathā | oṃ bhrūm | By taking up this dhāraṇī, one will retain the noble Avataṃsaka. 
One will be like someone who has written it and recited it. 
This completes “The Dhāraṇī for Retaining the Noble Avataṃsaka.”
