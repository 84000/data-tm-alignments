The Noble
Dhāraṇī to Uphold “The King of Samādhis Sūtra”
Homage to the Three Jewels.
namaḥ samanta­buddhānām aprati­hataśāsanānāṃ oṃ dhuna dhuna hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ svāhā!
By upholding this, one will have upheld The Noble King of Samādhis Sūtra.
Here ends the noble “Dhāraṇī to Uphold ‘The King of Samādhis Sūtra.’ ”
