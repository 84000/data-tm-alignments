{{milestone:{label:tr,id:translation}}}The Noble Dhāraṇī
Spoken by Mañjuśrī Himself
{{milestone:{label:1.1,id:UT22084-089-002-13}}}{{page:{number:1,id:UT22084-089-002-76,folio:F.13.a}}} Homage to the Three Jewels! Homage to noble Mañjuśrī!
namo ratna­trayāya | namo bhagavate dharma­dhātunigarjita­rājāya tathāgatāya arhate samyak­sambuddhāya | {{page:{number:2,id:UT22084-089-002-75,folio:F.13.b}}} namo mañjuśrīye kumārabhūtāya ||{{note:{index2,id:UT22084-089-002-17}}}
{{milestone:{label:1.2,id:UT22084-089-002-18}}}tadyathā | oṁ jñānāloke tri­ratna­vaṃśa­saṃdhāraṇi{{note:{index3,id:UT22084-089-002-19}}} bhagavati abhayaṃ{{note:{index4,id:UT22084-089-002-20}}} dādi abhayaṃ{{note:{index5,id:UT22084-089-002-21}}} datte me bodhicitta­vajra­paripālaya samantāva­bhāsena hana hana sarva­sattva­saṃtānapatitaṃ{{note:{index6,id:UT22084-089-002-22}}} kleśān ucchedaya{{note:{index7,id:UT22084-089-002-23}}} vara­lakṣaṇālaṃkṛtaśarīre{{note:{index8,id:UT22084-089-002-24}}} bodhisattva­śiśūn{{note:{index9,id:UT22084-089-002-25}}} paripālaya{{note:{index10,id:UT22084-089-002-26}}} buddhān ādeśaya{{note:{index11,id:UT22084-089-002-27}}} deva­nāga­yakṣa­gandharvāsura­garuḍa­kinnara­mahoragān{{note:{index12,id:UT22084-089-002-28}}} vaśe sthāpaya{{note:{index13,id:UT22084-089-002-29}}} abhayaṃ{{note:{index14,id:UT22084-089-002-30}}} dadati svāhā ||{{note:{index15,id:UT22084-089-002-31}}}
{{milestone:{label:1.3,id:UT22084-089-002-32}}}As for the benefits of this dhāraṇī, even a single recitation will purify the karmic obscurations accrued over a thousand eons,  and at the time of death one will behold noble Mañjuśrī.{{note:{index16,id:UT22084-089-002-33}}} One will also behold the body of noble Avalokiteśvara, his head adorned with a topknot. In short, the benefits of this dhāraṇī are limitless.
{{milestone:{label:1.4,id:UT22084-089-002-34}}}This concludes the noble dhāraṇī “Spoken by Mañjuśrī Himself.”
