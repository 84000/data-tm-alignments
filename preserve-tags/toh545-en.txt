{{milestone:{label:tr,id:translation}}}The Noble Dhāraṇī
Spoken by Mañjuśrī Himself
{{milestone:{label:1.1,id:UT22084-089-002-13}}} Homage to the Three Jewels! Homage to noble Mañjuśrī!{{page:{number:1,id:UT22084-089-002-76,folio:F.13.a}}}
namo ratna­trayāya | namo bhagavate dharma­dhātunigarjita­rājāya tathāgatāya arhate samyak­sambuddhāya |  namo mañjuśrīye kumārabhūtāya ||{{page:{number:2,id:UT22084-089-002-75,folio:F.13.b}}}{{note:{index2,id:UT22084-089-002-17}}}
{{milestone:{label:1.2,id:UT22084-089-002-18}}}tadyathā | oṁ jñānāloke tri­ratna­vaṃśa­saṃdhāraṇi bhagavati abhayaṃ dādi abhayaṃ datte me bodhicitta­vajra­paripālaya samantāva­bhāsena hana hana sarva­sattva­saṃtānapatitaṃ kleśān ucchedaya vara­lakṣaṇālaṃkṛtaśarīre bodhisattva­śiśūn paripālaya buddhān ādeśaya deva­nāga­yakṣa­gandharvāsura­garuḍa­kinnara­mahoragān vaśe sthāpaya abhayaṃ dadati svāhā ||{{note:{index3,id:UT22084-089-002-19}}}{{note:{index4,id:UT22084-089-002-20}}}{{note:{index5,id:UT22084-089-002-21}}}{{note:{index6,id:UT22084-089-002-22}}}{{note:{index7,id:UT22084-089-002-23}}}{{note:{index8,id:UT22084-089-002-24}}}{{note:{index9,id:UT22084-089-002-25}}}{{note:{index10,id:UT22084-089-002-26}}}{{note:{index11,id:UT22084-089-002-27}}}{{note:{index12,id:UT22084-089-002-28}}}{{note:{index13,id:UT22084-089-002-29}}}{{note:{index14,id:UT22084-089-002-30}}}{{note:{index15,id:UT22084-089-002-31}}}
{{milestone:{label:1.3,id:UT22084-089-002-32}}}As for the benefits of this dhāraṇī, even a single recitation will purify the karmic obscurations accrued over a thousand eons,  and at the time of death one will behold noble Mañjuśrī. One will also behold the body of noble Avalokiteśvara, his head adorned with a topknot. In short, the benefits of this dhāraṇī are limitless.{{note:{index16,id:UT22084-089-002-33}}}
{{milestone:{label:1.4,id:UT22084-089-002-34}}}This concludes the noble dhāraṇī “Spoken by Mañjuśrī Himself.”
