{{milestone:{label:tr,id:translation}}}The Noble Mahākāla Dhāraṇī:
A Cure for All Diseases and Illnesses
{{milestone:{label:1.1,id:UT22084-091-066-59}}}{{page:{number:1,id:UT22084-091-066-114,folio:F.202.a}}} Homage to all buddhas and bodhisattvas.
I will recite the dhāraṇī{{note:{index:2,id:UT22084-091-066-63}}} of Mahākāla, the great black being with sharp yellow fangs, the great fanged one who cures all diseases and illnesses. Wherever I direct my gaze, may I be free of disease and illness.{{note:{index:3,id:UT22084-091-066-64}}}
